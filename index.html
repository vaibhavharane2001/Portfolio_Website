1. Smart Canteen Automation System using Android.

Github-Link

The Smart Canteen Automation System Using Android is an innovative solution designed to streamline canteen operations and enhance the customer experience. Built using Java with Firebase for data storage, the app allows users to order food online, browse menus, place custom orders, and reserve tables seamlessly. It features secure online payments via Razorpay and generates unique order IDs for efficient tracking. This system reduces manual workload, optimizes operations, and provides a hassle-free dining experience for customers.

3. Portfolio Website.

Github-Link

A portfolio website is a personal platform designed to showcase your skills, projects, achievements, and professional experience. It serves as an online resume and a creative space where you can present your work to potential employers, clients, or collaborators. A well-designed portfolio website typically includes an introduction or "About Me" section that highlights who you are, your expertise, and your career goals. It also features a projects section that displays detailed information about your best work, including descriptions, technologies used, and links to live projects or repositories. Additionally, it often includes an overview of your technical and soft skills, as well as a contact section with options for visitors to get in touch, such as email, phone number, or social media links. A responsive design ensures the site looks great on all devices, from desktops to smartphones. Creating a portfolio website is essential for developers, designers, and professionals in creative fields, as it reflects your personal brand and provides a competitive edge in the job market.
